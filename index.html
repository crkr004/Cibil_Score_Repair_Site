What Our Company Does & Doesn't Do

Do's (What We Can Fix)

  • **Multiple Loans Remove** (for data mismatch/incorrect entries).
  • **Data Mismatch Correction** and Joint Loans Remove.
  • **Remove Unknown Loans & Credit Cards** showing in your CIBIL.
  • **Grow Your Score** if you have no loans/cards but still have a low score.
  • Clear problems when **payment was made but not updated** (closed/paid loan/card still showing open).

Don'ts (What We Can't Fix)

  • If you have **Outstanding Amount (OD)**, no one can help until you pay.
  • **Settlement and Written Off Amounts** require paying 100% to grow your CIBIL Score.
  • **Late Payments and DPD's** (Days Past Due) cannot be removed.
  • **Loan & Card Enquiries** (Hard Enquiries) cannot be removed. **Suggestion:** Don't inquire multiple times; kindly wait 3 months after a rejection.
